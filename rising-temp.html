Rising Temp

It is confirmed that human activity has severely impacted the Earth’s current warming pattern.

What can we do about it?

About the Project

Experience Website
Personal Project

The objective of this project is to showcase the ramifications of increasing global temperatures that have been identified by the United Nation’s report released Fall of 2019.

The interaction illustrates key solutions that help decrease the Earth’s current warming pattern. The slider pushes this concept further as the user scrolls through the series to reach O°.

The intent of this user interaction is to educate users and give them access to various communities who are working towards lowering the Earth’s temperatures.
